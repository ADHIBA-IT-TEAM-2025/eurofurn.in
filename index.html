About Eurofurn

We are one of the leading manufacturers of world class shutters in South India from the year 2000. Most of the raw materials are imported and converted into finished goods on the latest imported machines like Panel Saw, CNC, Membrane Press, and Edge Banding. Our productes include complete range of wardrobe, kitchen shutters, cornice, skirting, pelmets, glass frame shutters and panels. Eurofurn ensures the use of superior raw materials, processed with state of the art technology machines to produce international quality products.
